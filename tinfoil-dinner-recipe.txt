Tin Foil Dinners

Ingredients:

- Aluminum foil
- 2 potatoes
- 1 cup carrots
- 1/2 cup onions
- 1/2 cup peppers
- 1/4 lb ground beef patty
- 1/4 cup grated cheese
- salt
- pepper
- ketchup/BBQ sauce


Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

2) Lay aluminum foil in the table, about 18 inch sheet

3) Slice potatoes, carrots, onions, peppers and layer in that order in
center of foil.

4) Place meat patty on top of veggie pile, sprinkle with cheese, salt, and
pepper.

5) Fold edges of aluminum over food in center to cover and form a
sealed covering. Poke with fork a 2-3 times for ventillation.

6) Place dinner in oven for 45 min - 1 hour.

7) Remove from oven, let cool for 10 min, open, and enjoy. Top
with ketchup, BBQ sauce, or whatever else you like.
